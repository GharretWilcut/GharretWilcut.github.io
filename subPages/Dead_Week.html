Dead Week

About the Project

Me and my friends went and developed a Megaman inspired platformer based on a student from S&T going to school for an exam. I mainly worked on the AI enemies and some of the artwork. This was a great learning experience with Git being a big problem early on, we forgot to make a gitignore file which caused many merge conflicts but once that was resolved it went very smoothly.

The game features challenging platforming sections, enemy AI with different behavior patterns, and pixel art aesthetics inspired by classic Megaman games.
