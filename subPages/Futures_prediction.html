Predator Prey Simulation

About the Project

I created a simulation of predator-prey dynamics using an Entity Component System (ECS) architecture. The simulation models the interactions between predators and prey in an ecosystem, demonstrating concepts such as population dynamics, reproduction, and survival strategies. The overall goal of the project was to explore how simple rules governing individual behaviors can lead to complex emergent phenomena at the population level. As well as get me comfortable with ECS architecture.

This simulation was built as a stepping stone for further projects I have in the works right now.
